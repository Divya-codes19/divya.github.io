Welcome to my project presentation website. This MoonSpace Station is designed for research, exploration, and understanding cosmic events.

Major Space Events

Meteor Showers

Annual celestial events where meteors streak across the sky.

Solar Flares

Powerful bursts of energy from the Sun affecting space weather.

Comet Sightings

Rare appearances of icy bodies with glowing tails.

Moon Phases

New Moon

The Moon is between Earth and Sun, invisible from Earth.

Waxing Crescent

A thin crescent appears as the Moon grows larger.

Full Moon

The entire face of the Moon is illuminated.

Waning Crescent

The Moon shrinks back to a thin crescent before New Moon.

Eclipses

Solar Eclipse

Occurs when the Moon blocks sunlight, casting shadow on Earth.

Lunar Eclipse

Happens when Earth’s shadow falls on the Moon.
